Our Love Story

Our story started back when we were in college. We had been classmates since the beginning of our first year at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines as Electronics and Communications Engineering students in 2011. Honestly, we weren't that close at first and we had different circles of friends. The first time we talked was when we had a project in 2012, and we exchanged cell phone numbers. We didn't give any brief introductions or share much about ourselves initially; it was more like working on a class project together, and we both finished it. But because of that project, we started chatting continuously and talking to each other more often. And from then on, we began our magical journey together.
